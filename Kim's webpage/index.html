About us

Who are we?

We are a small company based in Leiden. We are working as a team of two, providing quality house cleaning services with the great attention to details to our fellow Leideners for almost 2 years. By providing service with a team of two people, you can make sure that your house will be clean in no-time! We offer cleaning maintenance service on weekly, by-weekly and monthly basis.

FAQ

Answers to frequently asked questions

Do I need to provide supplies?
The basic multi-purpose cleaner, solution for cleaning kitchen/bathroom, some cloths and floor cleaning solution is enough for us to start! Unfortunately, there is only two of us, riding bikes, so we can't bring our own supplies (for now!)
Do I need to be home?
If you work from home, we will be as quiet as possible! If you are away, we can arrange the possibility of key exchange.
Are pets a problem?
We love pets and they are never a problem! If your dog/cat is scared of the vacuum cleaner, we will be careful around them.
How do I cancel or reschedule?
Via call, text message or email.
Are there cancellation fees?
We update our schedule daily, but we can't adapt if you cancel 24h before the scheduled appointment. We can't find replacement in that short notice, so please let us know on time, because we will be forced to charge you for the appointment.
